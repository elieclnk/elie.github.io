EvoWalk: Wearable Device for Muscle Stimulation and Gait Analysis

A medical device that analyzes walking patterns in real-time and provides functional muscle stimulation at the right time for each patient.

  • Led the design of the real-time and post-processing algorithms for the wearable device
  • Developed the ML models that achieve less than 15% MAE across 10+ spatio-temporal metrics and 2 joint angles using a single IMU sensor
  • Led the design and development of the clinical trials: Received FDA approval early 2024
